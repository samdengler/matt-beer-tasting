A Few Small Beers

Matt's 50th Birthday Beer Tasting · February 28, 2026

  1. Cold Slice
    Inner Voice · American Lager · 4.5%
    Super crisp light lager. Corn and barley, naturally carbonated.
  2. Chimay Cinq Cents
    Scourmont Abbey · Belgian Tripel · 8%
    Trappist monks since 1862. Apricot, pear, peppery spice, dry finish.
  3. Fair Isle Lora
    Fair Isle Brewing · Berry Field Blend · 3.9%
    Wild-fermented berry field blend. Raspberry, boysenberry, tart, refreshing.
  4. Love is Real
    Inner Voice · Double IPA · 8%
    Mosaic, Nectaron, Simcoe Cryo. Dank citrus, hazy, pillowy mouthfeel.
  5. Mango Lassi Sunset
    Inner Voice · Smoothie Sour · 6.0%
    Mega fruited with mango puree and fresh coconut. Tropical, tart, creamy.
  6. Fonta Flora Coracoid
    Fonta Flora Brewery · Barrel-Aged Blend · 14%
    The birthday closer. Barrel-aged barleywine meets imperial stout. Chocolate raisin, vanilla, bourbon.
